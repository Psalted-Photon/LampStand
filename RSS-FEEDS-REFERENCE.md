## Notes

- **New Categories Added**: Middle East (4 feeds) and Positive News (3 feeds) are now active
- **Human Interest Removed**: Replaced by "Positive News" with 3 uplifting news sources
- **Politics Renamed**: "Politics" is now "World Politics" for clarity
- **Expanded Categories**: Technopoly now has 9 sources (up from 2), Science has 7 sources (up from 2)
- **No More Google News**: Replaced with direct RSS feeds from major news organizations for better image support
- **Image Support**: Most feeds now include images. Fallback images used when RSS doesn't provide them
- **Content Extraction**: Full article text is extracted on-demand from source URLs, cached in database
- **Feed Reliability**: BBC, NY Times, Guardian, NPR, and AP are highly reliable. Some smaller outlets may have inconsistent structures
- **Reuters Removed**: Reuters Agency feeds were serving ads instead of news
